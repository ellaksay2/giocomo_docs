Interaction: whole chunk of time when animal is visible in zone

- Start of interaction: same as start of first explore
- End of interaction: same as end of last explore
- interaction encompasses all behaviors

Explore: when the nose goes through the grate 

- stop explore bouts between enter/exit of grate
- question: do we need to separate different explore bouts during an interaction

Putative touch: when the nose of the interacting animal is BEHIND the head-fixed animal

- overlaps with explore - NOT mutually exclusive

Bite: animal is biting the grate

- follows explore
- does not overlap with explore - mutually exclusive events
- end of bite: when they pause biting (TBD)
