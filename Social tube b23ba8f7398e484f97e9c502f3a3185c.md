# Social tube

Materials

- [ ]  2 curved arm pieces (total 4 pieces)
- [ ]  2 center vessel pieces (top and bottom)
- [ ]  2 WIDE slotted rings
- [ ]  2 solid tubes
- [ ]  4 petri dishes (bottoms only)
- [ ]  1 large wire mesh 5x5 square
- [ ]  2 small metal circles
- [ ]  2 small wire pieces (1-1.5 inches)

Tools

- [ ]  Epoxy glue
- [ ]  Dremel + cone shaped dremel head
- [ ]  metabond

1. Glue bent arms together, attach the ring using epoxy glue (attach to tube using thinner side of slot)
2. Glue solid tubes with petri dish bottoms to bottom 
3. Glue top and bottom of center vessel together
4. Dremel the edges off the center vessel
5. Cut 5x5 square out of thick wire mesh, glue to “front” of center vessel
    1. make sure this is very straight and even, use a reference build so the mesh is identical across videos
    2. can use sandpaper to smooth out ends so they aren’t as sharp
6. Glue petri dish to bottom and back of center vessel 
7. Paint bottom white (more than half on outer arms, less than half on inner arms)
    1. acetone works to remove paint if painted too high
    2. use two coats for max opacity
8. Make dividers
    1. use metabond to build up 2 “bases” to attach wire to 
    2. cut small piece of wire to use as handle 

Notes:

- shave down white holders only slightly from the bottom
- attach handles to dividers using metabond
